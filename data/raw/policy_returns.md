## Shipping policy
GlowNest Nepal ships across Nepal with standard delivery in 2–5 business days. Express delivery is available in the Kathmandu Valley. Orders over NPR 25,000 qualify for free standard shipping where available.
## Return policy
Products may be returned within 30 days if they are unopened and in original condition. Opened beauty and skincare items can be exchanged based on hygiene and safety requirements.
## Refund policy
Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. GlowNest will send confirmation once the refund is processed.
## Warranty policy
Most beauty products include a manufacturer quality guarantee. If your item is eligible for warranty support, our team will help you complete the return or replacement process.
## Privacy policy
GlowNest collects personal information only to process orders, support service requests, and improve the shopping experience. Your data is secured with encryption and is not shared without permission.
## Payment methods
We accept eSewa, Khalti, credit and debit cards, bank transfer, and cash on delivery where available. All online payments are processed through secure, trusted payment gateways.
